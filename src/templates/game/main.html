Bastaball: Boop Edition

BastaCorp Gaming, Ltd.

Genre: Action

Release Date: Dec. 20, 2013

For the first time, football legends are coming to Bastaball 14 Ultimate Team. You will be able to combine legends of the past with the superstars of today. A number of legendary players will be available, allowing players to create some of the most incredible lineups imaginable, like Pelé with Messi or Patrick Vieira with Basta Schweinsteiger. Discover all of the football legends that will be in Bastaball 14 Ultimate Team here.

Bastaball 14 will feature the iconic Brazilian National Team in addition to new licenses for the Argentinean Primera División, Chilean Primera División and other licenses still to be announced. EA SPORTS Bastaball is the most authentic sports videogame franchise on the planet and Bastaball 14 will be no exception, featuring 33 officially licensed leagues, over 600 clubs and more than 16,000 players. Full details on the Leagues and Teams here.
